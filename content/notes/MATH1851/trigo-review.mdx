<Block variant="secondary" title="Trigonometric identities">
    1. $\sin^2x+\cos^2x=1$
    2. $\tan x=\frac{\sin x}{\cos x}$
    3. $\csc x=\frac{1}{\sin x}$
    4. $\sec x=\frac{1}{\cos x}$
    5. $\cot x=\frac{1}{\tan x}$
    6. $\sin 2x=2\sin x\cos x$
    7. $\cos 2x=\cos^2x-\sin^2x$
    8. $\tan 2x=\frac{2\tan x}{1-\tan^2x}$
    9. $\sin(x\pm y)=\sin x\cos y\pm\cos x\sin y$
    10. $\cos(x\pm y)=\cos x\cos y\mp\sin x\sin y$
    11. $\tan(x\pm y)=\frac{\tan x\pm\tan y}{1\mp\tan x\tan y}$
    12. $1 + tan^2x = sec^2x$
    13. $1 + cot^2x = csc^2x$
</Block>
